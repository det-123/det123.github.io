Talent Management and Performance Systems

The challenge

Most performance management systems fail because they are either too complex to sustain or too vague to drive real accountability, leaving managers without tools to differentiate performance, develop talent, or make defensible termination decisions.

Approach

I build performance systems, succession planning frameworks, and compensation structures that align talent decisions with business strategy and create clear expectations at every level. My approach focuses on systems that managers will actually use and that produce documentation capable of withstanding legal scrutiny when difficult employment decisions are necessary.
