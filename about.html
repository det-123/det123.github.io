Foundation

A decade of senior leadership that made all of it possible.

The range and precision of Dominique's work today was shaped by a decade in senior leadership at a national HR services organization — an environment that required advising across more than 1,100 client companies and 26,000 employees simultaneously, spanning industries, company sizes, and HR challenges of every kind.

She advanced from HR Manager to Director of HR Services to Senior Director of HR Training and Compliance — building and leading a team of 30+ HR professionals, serving as the internal authority on employment law and compliance, and developing the organization's training and development function from the ground up. That function contributed to a 75% reduction in departmental turnover and a 60% increase in internal promotions year over year.

In 2024, she transitioned to independent consulting to work with organizations at greater depth and focus. She is also open to the right full-time senior HR leadership opportunity.
